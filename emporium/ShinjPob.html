Shinji's problem

Hedgehog's dilemma

Jan. 22nd, 2022

After his mother's death, he was abandoned by his father and lived for 11 years with his sensei, until he was summoned to Tokyo-3 to pilot Unit-01 against the Angels. Though he start off alone, Shinji grows closer to those around him and learns to love himself.

Quote from Shinji:

“I dont care what happens to me. I dont care what happens to the world. But Im bringing Rei back.”
